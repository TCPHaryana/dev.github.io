GetFeatureInfo & geometry from WMS using Leaflet
  • Constraints: Enable CORS/ JSONP on GeoServer/ Jetty/ Tomcat/ Apache.
  • Input: Click anywhere on the map.
  • Output: FeatureInfo in a popup & highlights its geometry on the map.
  • Use Cases: Click no/ single/ multiple (by clicking on the boundary of any feature) features on the map.
